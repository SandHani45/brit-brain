Daily schedule

Morning

  • 7:00 - 8:00 Arrival & Free Play: Children engage in free play, building social and motor skills.
  • 8:00 - 8:30 Morning Circle: Group activities that foster participation and communication.
  • 8:30 - 10:30 Learning & Exploration: Educational activities focused on language, art, and sensory development.
  • 10:30 - 12:00 Outdoor Play: Physical activities to promote health and coordination.

Noon

  • 12:00 - 12:30 Snack Time: A healthy snack and social time.
  • 12:30 - 1:00 Storytelling & Quiet Time: Relaxing with stories to stimulate imagination.
  • 1:00 - 2:30 Creative Learning: Exploration through art, science, and role-play activities.
  • 2:30 - 4:00 Art & Craft: Creative projects that enhance fine motor skills..

Evening

  • 4:00 - 4:30 Snack & Social Interaction: Light snacks and socializing.
  • 4:30 - 5:30 Structured & Free Play: Group activities or independent play for decision-making.
  • 5:30 - 6:00 Reflection & Calm Down: Relaxing activities to wind down the day.
  • 6:00 - 7:00 Pick-Up: Parents receive a summary of the day's progress.
